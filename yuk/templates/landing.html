What's a yukmark?

A "yukmark" is a way to store the things you find on the internet quickly and painlessly. It's a lot like the bookmarks in your browser, but available from any computer, any browser, anywhere!

Features:
  • URL bookmarks with comprehensive tagging
  • Note-taking
  • Quote storage
